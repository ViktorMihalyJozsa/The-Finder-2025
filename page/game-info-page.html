Welcome to the Finders game !

How to play ?

Click on a square to reveal what is underneath it. The number tells you how many mines are in the adjacent squares. Use this information plus some strategy to avoid the mines. If you click on a mine, you lose the game. If you think a square is a mine, right-click it to place a flag on it. If you have flagged all the mines around a square, you can click on the square with both buttons to open the remaining adjacent squares.

How to win ?

Winning the game is a matter of strategy and luck. You need to use the numbers to your advantage and figure out where the mines are. If you think a square is a mine, flag it. If you think a square is safe, open it. If you open all the safe squares, you win the game.

How to lose ?

Losing the game is easy. All you have to do is click on a mine. If you do that, you lose the game. Try to avoid the mines by using the numbers to your advantage. If you think a square is a mine, flag it. If you think a square is safe, open it. If you open a mine, you lose the game.

How to play again ?

If you want to play again, click on the action button. This will reset the game and give you a new board to play on. You can also change the difficulty level by selecting a different option from the dropdown menu. The difficulty level determines the size of the board and the number of mines on it.

How to change the difficulty level ?

If you want to change the difficulty level, select a different option from the dropdown menu. The difficulty level determines the size of the board and the number of mines on it. The easy level has a 8x8 board with 7 mines, the medium level has a 10x10 board with 10 mines, and the hard level has a 12x12 board with 15 mines. If you want to play on a custom board, you can change the difficulty level to custom and then enter the size of the board and the number of mines on it.

Good luck and have fun !
