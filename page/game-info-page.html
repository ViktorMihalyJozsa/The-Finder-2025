Welcome to the Finders game !

How to play ?

Click on a square to reveal what's underneath. The number shows how many cell points are in the adjacent squares. Use this information, along with some strategies, to avoid cell points. If you click on a cell point, you lose the game. If you think a square is a cell point, right-click on it to place a flag on it. After you've marked all the cell points around a square, click on the square with both buttons to open the remaining adjacent squares.

How to win ?

Winning the game is a matter of strategy and luck. You have to turn the numbers to your advantage and guess where the cell points are. If you think a cell point is on a square, mark it. If you think a square is safe, open it. If you open all the safe squares, you win the game.

How to lose ?

It's easy to lose the game. You just have to click on a cell point. If you click on it, you lose the game. Try to avoid cell points by turning the numbers to your advantage. If you think a square is a cell point, mark it. If you think a square is safe, open it. If you open a cell point, you lose the game.

How to play again ?

If you want to play again, click the large button in the middle. This will reset the game and give you a new board to play on.

How to change the difficulty level ?

You can also change the difficulty level by selecting another option from the drop-down menu in the upper right corner. The difficulty level determines the size of the board and the number of cell points on it. The easy level features an 8x8 board with 7 cell points, the medium level features a 10x10 board with 10 cell points, and the hard level features a 12x12 board with 15 cell points.

Good luck and have fun !
